The attached data file (wu_moin_blayer_Re_theta_80_to_3000_2014.tar.gz) contains DNS statistics of the ``Boundary Layer Bypass Transition'' simulations reported by X. Wu, P. Moin and J.P. Hickey at the 66th Annual APS Division of Fluid Dynamics Meeting in Pittsburgh, PA, which was award the 2013 Milton van Dyke Gallery of Fluid Motion award whose report is due to be published in Physics of Fluid (2014).  Selected image sequences are also located in the images/ directory.
